The stance

Built on three commitments we don't compromise.

Most networks ask you to trust a company with your conversations and your identity. Beamhop is engineered so you don't have to.

European sovereignty

Your data lives on infrastructure you choose, under the jurisdiction you choose. No foreign platform owns the pipes — anyone can run a node, from a laptop in Almere to a rack in Frankfurt.

Data privacy

Messages are encrypted to their recipients and wrapped so even the nodes carrying them can't see who's talking or what's said. Privacy is the default state, not a setting you have to find.

Security by design

Every event is signed with keys that never leave your device and verified independently by every peer. There's no trusted middleman holding the master copy — so there's no master copy to breach.
